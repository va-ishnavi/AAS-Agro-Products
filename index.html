About AAS Agro Products

Delivering Premium Agro Products Across India & Worldwide

AAS Agro Products is a trusted manufacturer, supplier, and exporter of high-quality agricultural commodities based in Andhra Pradesh, India.

We specialize in Tamarind with Seeds, Tamarind without Seeds, Tamarind Flowers, Maize, Soapnuts, and Jowar. Every product is carefully sourced from trusted farmers, processed hygienically, and packed to maintain freshness, purity, and quality for domestic and international markets.

Our Commitment

We are committed to delivering premium-quality agro products through ethical sourcing, strict quality control, timely delivery, and long-term partnerships with customers across India and global markets.
